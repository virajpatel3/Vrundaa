About Vrundaa

A Lal Bazar trade house, built on steel and service.

Vrundaa is a Kolkata-based wholesaler, distributor and retailer of hotelware and kitchenware, operating out of Lal Bazar Street since 1994. The firm stocks and supplies the equipment a working kitchen runs on — chafing dishes, crockery, bone china, cutlery, bar tools and bakeware — to hotels, restaurants, caterers and banquet halls.

Three decades on the same street have built a counter that hospitality buyers return to: practical stock, bulk pricing, and dispatch that reaches kitchens well beyond West Bengal.
